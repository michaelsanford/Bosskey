📊 Initech Efficiency Shield

Are you tired of looking like you only do 15 minutes of real actual work a week? Do you need a screen full of moving telemetry, matrices, compiler registers, and cloud alerts to deter people from approaching your desk?

Bosskey compiles to a single self-contained desktop console executable. It does absolutely nothing of utility, queries no networks, and writes no files. It just looks spectacular.

  • 8 Plausible Dashboards covering Network, Cloud, Active Directory, databases, AI clusters, and mainframes.
  • Emergency Lockdown mode triggers a simulated red-alert quarantine wipe procedure.
  • Interactive keyboard controls to pause log scrolls, change update speed, or toggle dashboards.
Compile to Standalone Binary
dotnet publish -c Release -r win-x64 --self-contained true -p:PublishSingleFile=true -p:PublishTrimmed=false
